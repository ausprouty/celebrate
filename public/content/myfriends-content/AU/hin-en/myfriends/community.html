apanee khud kee yaatra dijain karen vishvaas samudaay mein baahar rahane ke lie hai yahaan ek rooparekha hai ki jab aap ek baar yaatra pooree kar lete hain to aap kisee bhee baibal ke maarg ke saath paalan kar sakate hain.

Ansh
  1. aapane is saptaah bhagavaan ka anubhav kaise kiya hai?
  2. Aap kisalie aabhaaree hai?
  3. aapako bhagavaan kee madad kee kya zaroorat hai?
  4. Ham aapakee aavashyakataon ke saath aapakee sahaayata kaise kar sakate hain?
  5. Ek doosare ke lie praarthana karo
Sameeksha
  1. Hamaaree aakhiree baithak ke baad se aapane kya abhyaas kiya hai?
  2. Ve log kaise hain jinake lie aap ja rahe hain? kya unhen sabase adhik madad milegee?
  3. Kya aap kisee ke saath bhagavaan anubhav saajha karane mein saksham the? kya aap unake saath praarthana karate the?
  4. In logon ke lie praarthana karo
Padhen
\
  1. maarg do baar jor se padhen
  2. samooh kee madad ke saath ek vyakti apane shabdon mein beetane ko punah likhata hai
  3. kya kuchh yaad kiya ya joda gaya tha?

Khojana
  1. Is maarg mein aapake lie kya khada hai?
  2. aapako kya pasand hai aur aap is disha mein kya pareshaan hain?
  3. Iis maarg se aapako bhagavaan aur logon ke baare mein kya bataaya gaya hai?
Laagu Kare

    • badalane ke lie ek vyavahaar?
    • daava karane ka vaada?
    • Ek paalan karane ke lie ek udaaharan hai?
    • aadesh ka paalan karane ke lie?
