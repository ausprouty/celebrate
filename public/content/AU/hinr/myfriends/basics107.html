JESUS FILM Yeeshu kee philm

 

Jesas philm dekhen jesusfilm.org/watch.htmlpar upalabdh hai)

 

Yah yeeshu ke jeevan, shiksha aur dukh kee sateek chitran hai. is philm ko dekhane ke lie aap teen tareeke tay kar sakate hain

  • Ek aitihaasik philm ke roop mein: is maamale mein philm mein dikhaaya gaya sab kuchh ek kahaanee ban jaata hai jo 2000 saal pahale hua tha aur aajakal hamaare lie koee praasangikata nahin hai.
  • Maanavata kee mukti ke roop mein: yadi aap is drshtikon se philm dekhate hain, to yeeshu pooree maanav jaati ke paapon ke lie maare gae the aur vaastav mein mujhe bahut chinta nahin hai kyonki main sirph arabon mein hoon.
  • Meree uddhaar kee keemat ke roop mein: yadi ham is drshy ko dekhane se dekhate hain ki yeeshu ne mere lie vyaktigat roop se janm liya hai, to ham vaastav mein kya hua, yah samajh paenge. yah apane aap ko baar-baar jaagarook karana mahatvapoorn hai ki yah philm amerika ke baare mein hai. kisee ne buraee kee taakat se mujhe mukt karane ke lie buree yaatana ka saamana kiya aur mujhe bhagavaan ka raasta chunane ke lie svatantr banaane ka avasar mila..

Tay karen ki aap kitanee philm ek saath dekhenge. yadi aapake paas pooree philm ke lie samay nahin hai, to ek ghante ke ank se shuroo hone par vichaar karen. yah aapako abhee bhee yarooshalem jaane aur marane se pahale yeeshu ke sikhaane aur chamatkaaron ke udaaharan dega
