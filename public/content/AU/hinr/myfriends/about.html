Meree mitr yaatra aapako aur aapake mitr ek-doosare ke saath aur bhagavaan ke saath majaboot bandhan banaatee hai

yaatra kee neenv par banaata hai:

  1. Apane doston ke baare mein dekhabhaal karana pata karen ki vaastav mein aapake dost kee duniya mein kya chal raha hai isamen "aap kaise hain?" se aage badhana shaamil hai aur isaka uttar jaanane ke lie "aap vaastav mein kaise hain?"
  2. Pyaar dikhana. Mere mitr (My Friends) mein ham matalab nahin hai bhaavuk ya romaantik vichaaron ko aap teevee par dekhate hain pyaar ka arth hai apane doston kee aavashyakataon ko apanee aavashyakataon ke oopar rakhana. yah unake sarvottam hit mein kaam kar raha hai, tab bhee jab yah asuvidhaajanak hai.
  3. Dua - praarthana ham bhagavaan ke saath judane ka tareeka hai hamaare kaee samasyaen hamaare lie bahut badee hain, taaki ham khud ko hal kar saken. sthaayee parivartan dekhane ke lie hamen bhagavaan ko drshtikon badalane ya paristhitiyon ko dekhane kee aavashyakata hai.

Yadi bhagavaan aapake lie mahatvapoorn hain to yahaan chaar kriyaen hain jinhen aap lena chaahate hain. ye cheejen aapake lie naya ho sakatee hain vah theek hai. yah ek yaatra hai aur yaatra par aap in kaaryon ke saath behatar honge agar aapako lagata hai ki bhagavaan aapake paas bahut door hain ya aapako nahin lagata ki vah aapake jeevan mein kuchh bhee kar raha hai, to aap is set par ja sakate hain.

shaayad aapake ek mitr aapake saath saajha karenge ki bhagavaan apane jeevan mein kya kar rahe hain baat suno. savaal poochho. yaad rakhen ve aapaka dost hain aapako ve jo bhee kahate hain, usase sahamat nahin hona chaahie.

  1. Saajha karen ki parameshvar aapake jeevan mein kya kar raha hai vishisht hona. apanee kahaaniyon ko saajha karen jo aapake mitron ke anubhavon ke saath kuchh samaan hain aur jinake saath unhen pahachaanana aasaan hoga. aapakee bhaavanaon ke baare mein tippaniyaan shaamil hone par aapake mitr behatar aapakee kahaanee ka paalan karenge.
  2. Apane doston ko bhagavaan ka anubhav karane do. jab unakee zaroorat hotee hai, to poochhen ki kya aap unake lie praarthana kar sakate hain. jab aap praarthana karate hain, to apanee aankhen khulen. har shabd samajhen jo har koee samajh jaega yeeshu ke naam se praarthana karo. yadi sthiti kee anumati deta hai, to apane dost ko aapake baad praarthana karane do. tab unase poochhen ki kya unhonne dekha ki bhagavaan ne praarthana ka koee javaab nahin diya.
  3. Apane mitr ko bhagavaan se kanekt karen unhen samajhane mein sahaayata karen ki ve door nahin hain aur bhagavaan unake saath judane ke lie intajaar kar rahe hain. aap unhen yah bataana chaah sakate hain ki aap yeeshu ke anuyaayee kyon bane. agar ve bhagavaan ke dost banane ke lie seekhana chaahate hain, to aap unhen yah rooparekha dikha sakate hain ya is chitr ko aakarshit kar sakate hain. this outline or draw this picture.
  4. Rileej. apane mitr ko apane doston ko bataane ke lie protsaahit karen ki ve parameshvar ke dost kaise ban sakate hain.

Ek baar apane do ya do se adhik mitr eeshvar kee kahaanee ko ek saath khojana chaahate hain:

  1. Samooh banaen apane doston se poochhen ki ve ek niyamit aadhaar par bhagavaan ke baare mein baat karane ke lie ek saath milana chaahate hain. aap saaptaahik, paakshik ya vishesh kaaryakramon ke lie mil sakate hain. ham darshaneey sthalon kee yaatra ke lie rooparekha ka prayog karane ka sujhaav dete hain: jeevan mudde
  2. Mool baaten khojen ek baar jab aapake paas teen se saat logon ka ek samooh hota hai jo ek saath eeshvar kee kahaanee ke maadhyam se jaana chaahate hain, to aap jarnee van: da besiks ke maadhyam se jaana shuroo kar sakate hain. Journey One: The Basics.
  3. Jashn. hamaare jeevan mein mahatvapoorn nirnayon ko un logon ke saath manaaya jaata hai jo hamaare lie mahatvapoorn hain. yeeshu ka anusaran karane ka nirnay bapatisma mein manaaya jaata hai yahaan ek bapatisma paartee ko vyavasthit karane ke baare mein kuchh sujhaav die gae hain, jahaan aap apane parivaar, mitron aur samudaay ko aamantrit kar sakate hain. how to organise a baptism party where you caninvite your family, friends and community.
  4. Laiv samudaay: yaatra do vishvaas ek hee yaatra ko saajha kar rahe doston ke saath milakar jeevit rahana behatar hai. yahaan ek rooparekha hai ki jab aap yaatra ek ko poora kar len, to aap kisee bhee baibal ke maarg ke saath anusaran kar sakate hain. outline that you can follow with any Bible passage once you have completed the Journey One.
