JESUS FILM


Watch the JESUS film (available at jesusfilm.org/watch.html)


This is an accurate depiction of the life, teaching and suffering of Jesus. There are three ways you can decide to watch this film:

  • As a historic film: In this case everything shown in the film becomes a story that happened 2000 years ago and has no relevance for us nowadays.
  • As the salvation of humanity: If you watch the film from this point of view, then Jesus died for the sins of the entire human race and it does not really concern me much since I am just one in billions.
  • As the price for MY salvation: If we watch the film from the point of view that Jesus suff ered for ME personally, then we will be able to understand what really happened. It is important to make ourselves aware again and again that this film is about US. Someone suffered the worst torture to free ME from the power of evil and to make me free to choose God’s way again.

Decide how much of the movie you will watch together. If you do not have time for the entire movie consider starting at the one hour mark. This will give still give you examples of Jesus teaching and miracles before He goes to Jerusalem to suff er and die.

Stop at 1:15

  1. What has impressed you about what Jesus has said or done so far?
  2. How has Jesus described His mission or purpose (see 1:14)
  3. What has Jesus forecast will happen to Him? (1:15)

Stop at 1:43

  1. What is Pilate’s attitude toward Jesus?
  2. Why does Pilate have Jesus punished?
After the film
  1. What does it mean to me that Jesus suffered so much for me?
