Hamaare jeevan mein mahatvapoorn nirnayon ko un logon ke saath manaaya jaata hai jo hamaare lie mahatvapoorn hain.Yeeshu ka anusaran karane ka nirnay bapatisma mein manaaya jaata hai.

Hamane vaartaalaap 10 mein bapatisma ke mahatv ke baare mein baat kee: jal

Aap ek bapatisma paartee ka aayojan kyon nahin karate jahaan aap apane parivaar, mitron aur samudaay ko aamantrit kar sakate hain? Isase bapatisma ko mahatv milega aur isake lie un logon ke lie ek achchha mauka diya jaega jo yeeshu par vishvaas nahin karate ki ve usase milen.

Yah veediyo klip dikhaatee hai ki yeeshu ne kis tarah bapatisma liya tha.

 

Yahaan kuchh prashn aur not hain jo aapakee bapatisma paartee kee yojana banaane mein madad kar sakate hain:

  • Aap kaun aamantrit karana chaahate hain?
  • In logon ke aane ke lie ek suvidhaajanak samay kab hoga?
  • Ye log aaraam se kahaan mahasoos karenge?
  • Bapatisma ke lie aapako paanee kahaan milega? taalaab? mahaasaagar? ek bapatismaatmak jharana? ek katoree?
  • Aap ko bapatisma dena kaun chaahata hai? kaise apane doston ke baare mein apane vishvaas yaatra mein saajha kiya hai?
  • Bapatisma lene se pahale aap shaayad apanee aadhyaatmik yaatra ke samooh ke hisse ko bataana chaahen. yadi aap likhate hain ki aap kya kahana chaahate hain to aap adhik aaraam kar sakate hain
  • Kya aap kisee aur ko bapatisma ke mahatv ke baare mein kuchh kahana chaahate hain?
  • Baibal kahatee hai ki hamen logon ko "pita aur putr aur pavitr aatma ke naam par" bapatisma dena chaahie.
  • Aap apane bapatisma ke baad tauliya ke saath sukha sakte hai aur kapade badal sakate hain
  • Kya aap bhojan kee seva karana chaahate hain? kis prakaar ka?
  • Aap apane doston ko apane bapatisma ke baare mein kya sochenge? inamen se kaun yeeshu ke baare mein adhik jaanana chaahate hain?
